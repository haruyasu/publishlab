TikTok Video Publisher

A web application that allows you to upload and publish video content directly to your TikTok account through a secure, authorized connection using the official TikTok API.

Secure Authentication

Log in securely with your TikTok account using OAuth 2.0 without sharing your TikTok password.

Video Upload

Upload video files directly from your browser. Supports MP4, WebM, and MOV formats.

Direct Publishing

Publish videos directly to TikTok with captions, hashtags, and privacy settings.

How It Works

1

Connect Your TikTok Account

Click the login button and authorize publishlab to access your TikTok account via the official TikTok OAuth flow.

2

Upload Your Video

Select or drag-and-drop a video file from your device. Add a caption and hashtags for your post.

3

Publish to TikTok

Configure your content and privacy settings and publish. Your video will be posted directly to your TikTok account.
